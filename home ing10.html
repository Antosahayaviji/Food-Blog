Saag Paneer Receipe

A rich and creamy, green-coloured gravy recipe prepared mainly from palak and fenugreek leaves.
It hails from the popular Kashmiri cuisine, and it is one of the delicacy recipes prepared during the festival season or any special occasion.
It tastes good when served with roti or naan, but can also be served with a choice of rice.



INGREDIENTS NEEDED:

  1. Fenugreek leaves
  2. Water
  3. Salt
  4. Paneer
  5. Turmeric
  6. Cinnamon
  7. Ginger
  8. Cream

Step 1: Firstly in a large kadai heat 2 tbsp mustard oil and saute spices.

Step 2: Further add ¼ tsp turmeric, ½ tsp garam masala, ½ tsp coriander powder, 1 tsp fennel powder, ½ tsp ginger powder, ½ tsp sugar and ¾ tsp salt.

Step 3: Saute on low flame till the spices turn aromatic. Now add prepare methi palak puree.

Step 4: Cook for 3 minutes stirring continuously. Furthermore, add 1 cup of water

Step 5: Adjust consistency by adding water as required and now add 2 tbsp cream and mix well.

Step 6: Also, add 30 small cubes paneer and ½ cup grated paneer.

Step 7: Mix well and simmer for 5 minutes and now add 1 tsp kasuri methi and mix well.

Step 8: Finally, serve methi chaman with garlic naan / roti.


FOR EXTRA FLAVOUR

For more delicious, add some cream in the top.
